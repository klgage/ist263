Welcome to the World of True Crime

True crime is a genre exploring real-life criminal cases. Often shocking and mysterious, documentaries, podcasts, or YouTube videos have captivated individuals worldwide. But what exactly is true crime? True crime is real events, sometimes unresolved, that display the darker side of humanity. Notorious serial killers or unsolved mysteries provide a window into the processes of the justice system, law enforcement, and human psychology.

Within the last decade, true crime has substantially increased in popularity, due to the spike of streaming platforms like podcasts and YouTube channels. Widespread discussions about justice and ethics are sprout from these stories. True crime offers more than just entertainment, but a way to understand fear, safety, and the world outside of our own.
